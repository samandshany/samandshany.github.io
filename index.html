What's a bush dance?

Bush dances are an outback-style celebration for the whole family. The band will lead us in variety of fun songs involving the whole group. And best part is - you don't need to know how to dance! The band will explain every song, and we'll all be learning as we go.

Music
The band will play classic Australian sounds of the lagerphone, cow bell and percussion instruments, along with fiddle, guitar and drums.

Dancing
By the end of the night you'll get to know traditional and modern dances such as Strip the Willow, Heel and Toe Polka, Waltzing Matilda, and the Waves of Bondi.

What should I wear?

Think casual 'country' style.

Guys - jeans and a chequered shirt goes well. Jandals (thongs) are not recommended!

For girls, have some fun! A twirly dress, or jeans and a shirt. Just remember - no heels! Flats all around.
