What's a bush dance?

Bush dances are a classic Aussie celebration involving traditional folk dances. The special bush band will lead us in variety of fun songs involving the whole group, children too!

Here's a little video of a small bush dance to give you a feel for the style of music and dancing:

By the end of the night you'll know a range of traditional and modern dances such as Strip the Willow, Heel and Toe Polka, Waltzing Matilda, and the Waves of Bondi.

What if I can't dance?

The best part is - you don't need to know how to dance! Everything happens in a group. The band will explain every song, and we'll all be learning as we go. If you're really worried, you can simply sit and enjoy watching.

What should I wear?

Clothes - Dress comfy and relaxed. Casual shirts for guys and skirts for girls are fun! But whatever is comfortable for you.

Shoes - Make sure you wear flat, comfortable shoes which are not too loose.
