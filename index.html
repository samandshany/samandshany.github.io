What's a bush dance?

Bush dances are a classic Aussie celebration involving traditional folk dances. The band will lead us in variety of fun songs involving the whole group. By the end of the night you'll get to know traditional and modern dances such as Strip the Willow, Heel and Toe Polka, Waltzing Matilda, and the Waves of Bondi.

What if I can't dance?

The best part is - you don't need to know how to dance! Everything happens in a group. The band will explain every song, and we'll all be learning as we go. If you're really worried, just sit the first one out and watch.

What should I wear?

Clothes - Dress comfy and relaxed. Casual shirts for guys and skirts for girls are fun! But whatever is comfortable for you.

Shoes - Make sure you wear flat, comfortable shoes which are not too loose.
