What's a bush dance?

Bush dances are a classic Aussie celebration with lots of traditional folk dances. The bush band will lead us in a variety of fun dances involving the whole group, children too!

Here's a little video of a small bush dance to give you a feel for the style of music and dancing:

By the end of the night we'll get through a range of traditional and modern dances such as Strip the Willow, Heel and Toe Polka, Waltzing Matilda, and the Waves of Bondi.

What to expect

Here's a few details you might find helpful:

  • Most of the night will involve either dancing or eating, with a few words at the end.
  • Kids are welcome! And we'll have some special dances for them.
  • We have a large venue so there will be table seating for everyone, as well as a big area for the dancing.
  • You don't have to dance - you can sit and watch the fun instead!

What should I wear?

Clothes - Dress casual and relaxed. We suggest shirts and jeans, or if you're a girl you might prefer a fun skirt for dancing. But whatever is comfortable for you.

Shoes - Make sure you wear flat, comfortable shoes which are not too loose.
